REMOTE RECORDING

How It Works

Remote recording has revolutionised the way musicians collaborate, allowing them to create and share music easily despite geographical barriers.

As travel costs rise and schedules become more hectic, artists can leverage advanced technology and online platforms to write and produce music in real-time, enabling remote collaborations that allow artists to work together from different locations while still achieving high-quality results. This does not only enhances accessibility, but also opens the door to a diverse range of influences and ideas.

THE PROCESS

Get In Contact

- Get in touch via email or phone to discuss the details of your project.

- I'll provide a quote for the session based on your needs for the project.

- Once the details and pricing are agreed on, you'll send over a folder containing a guide track (with drums) and a drumless copy of the track. Be sure to add any notes or references you require for your track.

Recording

- If there's a specific feel you're looking to achieve, I'll aim to recreate that based on the guide track. Otherwise I will work on creating a drum part that best compliments the feel of the track.

- Once i've recorded a take i'm happy with, I'll send you a rough mix with the track to preview. I will also work with you to make any changes you wish to make up to a maximum of 3 revisions.

Download Your Files

- After discussion, revisions and you're 100% happy with the take, I'll prep the drum stems/files and go through final details to ensure everything is in place.

- I'll send over an invoice for the project. This will include details for payment transfer.

- Once payment has been received, I'll send over a download link to the drum stems/files for you to import into any DAW of your choice.
